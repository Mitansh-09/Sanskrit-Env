How to use
  • Glossary Anchoring — Choose the correct domain-specific meaning of a Sanskrit technical term (e.g. agni means different things in Ayurveda vs. philosophy).
  • Sandhi Resolution — Identify the correct phonological split of a compound word into its component parts.

  • Referential Coherence — Track pronouns and implicit subjects across a multi-verse passage (multiple steps per episode).
